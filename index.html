What we do

Utilizing HTML5, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript, I build fully functioning websites for major and local businesses, as well as entrepeneurs. Here's the process:

Consultation

The first step in any collaboration between client and web developer is the consultation. Together we will create a list of needs and wants per page of your website.

Proposal

A proposal will be completed, including three mock-ups for website layout/design.

Creation

With regular updates to my clients, I will work to build the website's design, functionality, and perform tests of quality assurance for a smooth launch.

Launch

We've arrived to the most exciting part! Your site will be launched on your desired platform and depending on the functionalities, I will walk you through how to easily manage your site post-contract.
